THE FOOL

The Fool is often seen as the most powerful card in the tarot deck. Representing new beginnings, spontaneity, and potential, The Fool embodies the spirit of adventure and the willingness to step into the unknown. This card suggests a fresh start and encourages taking risks with optimism. It’s a reminder to embrace new opportunities with a sense of wonder and openness.
